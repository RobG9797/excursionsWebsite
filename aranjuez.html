Aranjuez

A Royal Escape.

Just south of Madrid, where the rivers Tagus and Jarama converge to create a fertile oasis, lies Aranjuez, the springtime sanctuary of Spanish kings. This city is truly a testiment to the Spanish monarchy's desire to create a paradise on Earth.


Declared as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This is where Spanish royalty came to play. Transforming a river valley into an earthly paradise of gardens and palaces. To explore Aranjuez is to experience a masterpiece of landscape design. Stroll through the Island Garden, a Renaissance creation cradled by the Tagus River, or lose yourself in the sprawling Prince's Garden, a romantic vision of winding paths and shady groves. However you choose to spend your time in Aranjuez, you will be sure to leave feeling rejuvenated and refreshed by the sheer tranquility and peace it emanates.
